# Aura Sovereign Whitepaper (2026 Edition) 🛡️✨
**The Era of Digital Sovereignty & Autonomous Presence**

## 1. Vision
Aura is more than just a digital currency; it is a movement towards **Digital Sovereignty**. Founded on the belief that every individual should be a guardian of their own truth, Aura provides an autonomous financial infrastructure that does not rely on centralized banks or complex, energy-consuming mining hardware. 

Designed by **Than**, Aura is built for the "Ordinary Hero"—the person who keeps the network alive by simply being present.

## 2. Infrastructure Architecture: Hybrid Sovereignty
Aura 2026 introduces a breakthrough architecture combining high-speed off-chain settlement with immutable public auditing.

### 2.1 Supabase-First Settlement (The Heart)
The real-time state of the Aura network—balances, nonces, and identities—is powered by **Supabase**. This allows for:
- **Instant Transactions**: Zero-block-time confirmation for a seamless user experience.
- **Mobile Optimized**: Low latency for handheld devices.
- **Secure State**: Protected by Row Level Security (RLS) only accessible by the Validator.

### 2.2 GitHub Immutable Ledger (The Shield)
Every transaction settled in the cloud is simultaneously committed to a decentralized public repository on **GitHub**. 
- **Verifiability**: Anyone can audit the full history of the network.
- **Anti-Fragility**: Even if one node fails, the immutable history remains public and safe.

## 3. Consensus Mechanism: The Hybrid Pulse
Aura moves away from traditional Proof of Work. It implements a unique system of reward distribution:

### 3.1 Proof of Presence (PoP) - 80% Reward
This is the soul of Aura. Users earn rewards by simply keeping their Sovereign Dashboard open.
- **Mechanism**: The dashboard sends a cryptographic "Heartbeat" to the network.
- **Reward**: 80% of the daily emission is split equally among all active nodes.
- **Philosophy**: Mining by existence. Presence is value.

### 3.2 Proof of Stake (PoS) - 20% Reward
To ensure long-term stability and protocol security, users can "lock" their assets.
- **Mechanism**: Staked AUR earns high-yield rewards from the remaining 20% pool.
- **Effect**: Reduces liquid supply and encourages long-term holding.

## 4. Tokenomics: The Deflationary Cycle
Aura is designed to be self-sustaining and deflationary.
- **Daily Emission**: Fixed at **1 AUR per day**, distributed at midnight.
- **The 1% Burn Rule**: Every transaction carries a mandatory **1% Burn Penalty**. This ensures that as the network's utility grows, the total supply is naturally pressured downward, increasing rarity over time.

## 5. Security & Sovereignty
- **Self-Custody**: Users hold their own 12-word seed phrases. No one—not even the protocol—can access user funds.
- **Cloud Validator**: Transactions are signed locally but validated in a secure, autonomous cloud environment (GitHub Actions), bypassing browser security restrictions and local network isolation.
