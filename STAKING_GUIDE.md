## 🔱 The Concept: Sovereign Proof of Stake (PoS)

Aura uses a **Proportional-Share Minting** mechanism. By "locking" your AUR tokens into the Sovereign Vault, you provide the economic security needed for the network to thrive.

### 1. The Fixed Reward Pool
Every day at **00:00 UTC**, the system mints exactly **1 AUR**. 
- This 1 AUR is a distributed among all stakers.
- **Your Reward** = `(Your Staked Amount / Total Network Staked) * 1 AUR`.
- If you own 10% of the total staked AUR, you receive 0.1 AUR every day.

### 2. How to "Stake"
Staking in Aura is as simple as a few clicks on your Dashboard:
1. **Login**: Access your Celestial Dashboard with your seed phrase.
2. **Open Vault**: Click on the **"Stake / Unstake"** modal.
3. **Lock Assets**: Enter the amount of AUR you wish to lock and sign the transaction.
4. **Validation**: Your transaction is validated in ~2 seconds by the Sovereign Validator.
5. **Autopayout**: Midnight passes (UTC), and your rewards are automatically added to your balance.

### 2. Self-Custody
Your assets are yours alone.
- **Seed Phrases**: You control your 12-word recovery phrase.
- **No Lockups**: While staking earns rewards, you can "Unstake" at any time. Unstaking is instant, but you will lose your reward share for the upcoming cycle.

### 3. Verification
You can track your staking status and cumulative rewards in the **Sovereign Activity** feed on your Dashboard. Every distribution is also recorded on the public GitHub ledger for 100% transparency.

## 🛡️ Security of the Vault

- **Atomic Protection**: Staking operations use Atomic RPC functions that ensure your balance and staked status are updated simultaneously.
- **EIP-191 Signatures**: Every stake operation is signed locally and verified cryptographically.
- **The 1% Burn**: To keep the ecosystem healthy, every stake/unstake operation contributes to a **1% burn penalty**, ensuring the token remains deflationary as the community grows.
